The Billie Holiday Symposium

At Hunter College's Roosevelt House

To celebrate the artistry of Billie Holiday and the publication of Bitter Crop: The Heartache and Triumph of Billie Holiday's Last Year, a new book by Paul Alexander

February 8, 2024

About The Symposium

The Billie Holiday Symposium at Hunter College is funded by the college and run by a student committee. The event is led and supervised by Paul Alexander and student committee chair, Julie Rosenberg.

The students on our committee are:

  • Julie Rosenberg
  • Nadia Martinez
  • Chynna Slaughter
  • Sara Mahraoui
  • Peri Micheva
  • Michael Dmitrenko
  • Emma Tramontana
  • Stevie Dattomo
  • Hannah Busch
  • Kristina Xhangolli
  • Diana Lopez
  • Olivia Tymon
  • Mykaya Borden
  • Neil Sawhney

Bitter Crop: The Hearthache and Triumph of Billie Holiday's Last Year is a new book by Paul Alexander, which is being released on February 8th. Copies will be for sale at the event.
